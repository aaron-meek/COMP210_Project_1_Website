Computers on the job

Before computers were introduced into our world, certain tasks like complex calculations were performed by hand, with the aid of tables and basic mechanical devices. Engineering drawings were, literally drawings done by engineers. Today, we are provided with many electronic devices that both simplify and speed up the process. This both increase the number of jobs available, and reduced it. Engineers could now hire drafting technitions who are tained in using drafting software to create engineering draings, and large calculations could quickly be handled by the machines themselves. As a whole, we've created all sorts of computing and data centers that enable us to interact with the world, and complete large scale projects that were once unfeasible. This had a compounding effect, as the better we became at measuring, analyzing, organizing, and storting things, the better we became at pushing the limits of our current technology at any given time.
